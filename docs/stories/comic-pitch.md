# Comic Pitch

## What is a Pitch?

A comic pitch is a proposal submitted by a creator to the Whaleden community. It outlines a story concept, visual style, and the creative direction of a potential comic franchise. The community then votes with their support — financially and socially — to determine which pitches move forward into full production.

## How it Works

1. **Create your pitch** — Submit a story concept with title, synopsis, genre, and sample art
2. **Community review** — Fans browse active pitches and choose which ones to back
3. **Funding threshold** — When a pitch reaches its funding goal, production begins
4. **Launch** — The comic is published on the platform and backers receive exclusive benefits

## Funding

!!! info "Community-driven funding"
    Pitches are funded directly by fans. There is no corporate investment required. Your story lives or dies by community support.

Funding mechanics:

- Creators set a minimum funding goal before submitting
- Fans pledge support during the active pitch window
- If the goal is not met, pledges are returned
- If the goal is met, production kicks off and backers receive early access

## FAQ

**Who can submit a pitch?**
Any registered artist on the platform. Apply via the **Account → Artist Program** section.

**Is there a submission fee?**
No. Pitching is free for approved artists.

**How long does a pitch stay active?**
Pitch windows are set by the creator, typically 30–60 days.

**What happens after funding?**
The creator enters production and delivers chapters on an agreed schedule. Backers receive chapter access as they are released.
