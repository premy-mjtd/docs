# Comics

Browse and read comics available on the Whaleden platform.

## How to Access Comics

Comics are available through your **Library** in the account dashboard at <https://collect.whaleden.com/>.

!!! tip "Color = available to read"
    Previews display in black and white. Once purchased or unlocked, the full color version becomes available.

## Comic Types

### 🆓 Free Comics

Selected Golden Age comics are available at no cost to all registered users. These are classics made freely accessible to grow the community.

### 🛒 Purchasable Comics

New and independent comics are available for purchase. Each purchase grants permanent access to all released chapters.

### 🎟️ Pitch-backed Comics

Comics that originated from funded pitches. Early backers receive chapters ahead of general availability.

## Reading Experience

Comics are readable directly in the browser — no additional app required. The reader supports:

- Page-by-page navigation
- Full-screen mode
- Mobile-friendly layout
